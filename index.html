Explore Rajasthan

🏰

Jaipur - The Pink City

UNESCO World Heritage Site

The perfect blend of royal charm and modern energy. Explore the majestic Amber Fort, iconic Hawa Mahal with 953 windows, and the grand City Palace.

💧

Udaipur - The City of Lakes

The Venice of the East

Romantic and serene. Enjoy boat cruises on Lake Pichola, marvel at the City Palace's mosaic artwork, and visit the beautiful Saheliyon Ki Bari gardens.

🐪

Jaisalmer - The Golden City

Sands of Time

Experience the "Living Fort," explore intricate Patwon Ki Haveli, and enjoy Desert Safaris at Sam Sand Dunes with cultural performances under the stars.

🕌

Jodhpur - The Blue City

Gateway to Thar

Discover the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, the beautiful Jaswant Thada marble cenotaph, and vibrant Clock Tower Bazaar for authentic handicrafts.

Frequently Asked Questions

🌡️ What is the best time to visit Rajasthan? +

The best time to visit Rajasthan is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for sightseeing. During these months, temperatures range from 10°C to 27°C, making it ideal for exploring forts, palaces, and desert safaris.

Winter (November-February): Perfect for desert camping and outdoor activities.

Summer (April-June): Very hot (up to 45°C), but fewer tourists and better hotel deals.

🎒 What should I pack for a Rajasthan trip? +

Clothing: Light cotton clothes for day, warm layers for evenings (winter), comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, and a hat or scarf for sun protection.

Essentials: Sunscreen (SPF 50+), water bottle, camera, power bank, and any personal medications.

Tip: Dress modestly when visiting temples and religious sites. Carry a shawl or stole.

🍛 What kind of food can I expect? +

Rajasthani cuisine is rich, flavorful, and diverse! Must-try dishes include:

Dal Baati Churma: Traditional Rajasthani staple - lentil curry with baked wheat balls and sweet crumble.

Laal Maas: Spicy red meat curry (for non-vegetarians).

Ghevar & Mawa Kachori: Famous sweet treats.

We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and dietary restrictions. Just inform us in advance!
